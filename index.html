About Me

I am Sahiti.
I am a Computer Science Engineer by education and Designer at heart.

I did bachelors in Computer Science and Engineering from Ramachandra College of Engineering, Eluru.
I have been intersted in arts and crafts right from my childhood. That has naturally slid my heart towards graphic design.

You can generally see me fiddling with my laptop creating few eye-hurting graphics.

While I am free, you can notice me make bursting peoples eardrums with my songs.
Oh! Did I mention that I am a classical singer?

Warning: Navigate to the other parts of this website at your own risk. I am not responsible for any broken hearts or burst eyes or both! Lol!!

Jokes apart, I love designing interfaces those are easy to use and are a candy to eyes.
My goal is to make sure that the users do not think while using interfaces that I design. My contentment with an app design is inversely proportional to amount of brain activity a user has while consuming that app.
